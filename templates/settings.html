How to export cookies from your browser

  1. Install the Get cookies.txt LOCALLY extension for Chrome, or the equivalent for Firefox.
  2. Make sure you are logged into YouTube in that browser.
  3. Navigate to youtube.com.
  4. Click the extension icon and choose Export for youtube.com.
  5. Save the downloaded youtube.com_cookies.txt file and upload it above.
Cookies expire. If downloads of gated content start failing again, re-export and re-upload. Your cookies give the same access as your Google account â€” keep the file private.
